Montfort School, Kattur – Trichy is managed by the Christian Religious Congregation of the Montfort Brothers of St. Gabriel known for their unique service in the field of education mission around the globe. Montfort school was established in the year 2006 and now has seen unprecedented growth nurturing more than 4000 students from all walks of life. Following the CBSE syllabus the school dedicates itself to the holistic development of the human person. It is not an exaggeration to call it the real TEMPLE OF LEARNING.
